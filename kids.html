Kids' Shoe Size Guide

How to Measure Your Child's Foot

  1. Place your child's foot on a piece of paper.
  2. Trace around the foot with a pencil.
  3. Measure the length from heel to toe.
  4. Use our size chart below to find the right size.
Age US Size Foot Length (inches) Foot Length (cm)
1 year 5 4 5/8 11.7
2 years 7 5 1/8 13
3 years 9 5 5/8 14.3
4 years 10 6 1/4 15.9
5 years 12 6 5/8 16.8
6 years 13 7 17.8

Growing Feet
